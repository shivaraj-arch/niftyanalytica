Nifty Analytica ("we," "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in our mobile app, Nifty Analytica (the "App"), and on our public website. By using the App or website, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal, non-personal data:

  • Device Information: Device type and OS version to ensure app functionality.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized analytics such as feature usage to improve the app and website experience.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymized error data to diagnose and fix technical issues.

We do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name or email unless you choose to contact us directly.

How We Use Data

We use data to:

  • Deliver stock market analytics through internet connectivity.
  • Enhance app and website performance and user experience.
  • Fix technical issues and improve reliability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data, except in these cases:

  • Analytics Providers: Anonymized data may be processed by services such as Firebase. See Firebase Privacy.
  • Legal Obligations: If disclosure is required by law.

Data Security

We use reasonable measures, including encryption where applicable, to protect data. No system is fully secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Your Choices

You can disable analytics through device settings where supported, or contact us if you have questions about data handling.

Children's Privacy

The App and website are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any future updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email admin@niftyanalytica.com.
